# Getting started

Quickly set up the app and see it in action. This guide walks you through installation, localization, theme setup, and assigning your first EPREL numbers.



### 1. Installing the app



You can install the EU Energy Label app directly from the Shopify App Store:



1. On the app listing page, click **Install**.
2. 2\. In your Shopify admin, confirm app permissions and click **Install** again to authorize.
3. 3\. After installation, select your subscription plan.

The app will then be available on the Apps page in your Shopify admin.



### 2. Localization



Go to **Apps → EU Energy Label → Settings** and set your preferred language under **Product sheet language**.



This setting determines the language of the product information sheet (PDF) and the link text displayed next to the energy badge.



Supported languages: English (Default), Deutsch, Français, Español, Italiano, Nederlands, Polski, Svenska, Dansk, Suomi, Ελληνικά, Български, Čeština, Română, Hrvatski, Magyar, Eesti, Lietuvių, Latviešu, Slovenčina, Slovenščina, Malti, Português.



### 3. Enabling the app embed in your theme



To activate the energy labels across your storefront:



1. Go to **Online store → Themes** in your Shopify admin.
2. 2\. Click **Customize** next to the theme you want to use.
3. 3\. In the editor, open the **App embeds** panel (bottom left).
4. 4\. Toggle **Energy Label** to enable it.
5. 5\. Save your changes.

### 4. Adding the Energy Label app block to the Product page



Go to **Online store → Themes → Customize** and open the Product template. Click **Add section**, switch to the Apps tab, and select **Energy Label**.



Drag the app block into place — ideally near the product price or buy button. Adjust the block’s appearance using the available styling options.



**Link text** allows you to customize the wording for the Product Information Sheet text that appears next to the energy badge — it will override the default value.



### 5. Assigning EPREL numbers to products



To display the correct energy badge, each energy-efficient product must have its EPREL number assigned.



1. Go to **Products** in your Shopify admin.
2. 2\. Open the product. Scroll to the **Product metafields** section and locate the **EPREL Registration Number** field.
3. 3\. Enter a valid EPREL number and click **Save**.

Once saved, the storefront will display the official energy badge. Clicking the badge opens a pop-up with the full energy label, while the product information sheet link downloads the localized fiche in PDF format.



#### Assigning EPREL numbers in bulk



1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Products** and select all relevant items. Click **Bulk edit**.
2. 2\. Click **Columns** in the top right corner. Scroll to **Metafields**, then check **EPREL Registration Number** to make the column visible.
3. 3\. Enter the correct EPREL number for each product in the newly visible field. Click **Save**.

> **Tip:** The energy badge only renders on products where the **EPREL Registration Number** metafield is filled in. If the metafield is empty, no badge is displayed — this is expected behaviour, not a bug. Make sure to assign EPREL numbers to all products that need to display a label.
