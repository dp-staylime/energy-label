# Variants Support

The app fully supports Shopify variants, allowing you to display a variant-specific energy class badge, full energy label, and product information sheet based on the selected option.



To enable this, add the EPREL Registration Number to the relevant **variant metafield** in your Shopify admin:



1. Go to **Products** in your Shopify admin.
2. 2\. Open an existing product and go to its variants.
3. 3\. For each variant, locate the **EPREL Registration Number** metafield.
4. 4\. Enter the correct EPREL number and click **Save**.

> 📸 SCREENSHOT: Shopify admin — variant metafields panel with EPREL Registration Number field

### Important notes



**Where variant-based energy badges appear:**



* Product pages
* \- Cart drawer
* \- Cart page

**Where they do NOT appear:**



* Collection pages
* \- Search results

These templates typically use ‘Color’ as a swatch option and don’t reflect variant-level energy efficiency.



If you want energy badges to show in Collection and Search, we recommend also setting the EPREL Registration Number **at the product level**. This way:



* **Product-level badge** → visible in Collection and Search
* \- **Variant-level badge** → shown on the Product page, Cart drawer, and Cart after a variant is selected

> **Note:** The Manual Efficiency Class for Legacy Products feature is **not supported** for variants.
