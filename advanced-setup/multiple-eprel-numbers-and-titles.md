# Multiple EPREL Numbers & Titles

For complex products like kitchens or appliance bundles, the app supports displaying **multiple energy class badges** on the product page — each with its own **custom title**.

To enable this feature, enter all EPREL numbers into the **EPREL Registration Number** metafield at the **product level**, using the following format:

```
1576181 [Dishwasher Siemens SN65YX00CE], 160567 [Oven Bosch HVA331BS0], 1776798 [Refrigerator Liebherr IRBPbsci 5170 BioFresh]
```

* The **number** is the EPREL registration number for the product.
* The **title** in **square brackets** is a custom label that will be displayed alongside the corresponding energy badge.
* Use a **comma** to separate each EPREL number and title pair.
